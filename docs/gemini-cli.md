
Use your Gemini CLI account with Zo

[Gemini CLI](https://geminicli.com/) is Google's agentic coding tool, available on all [Gemini plans](https://geminicli.com/plans/?category=individual) including free. If you have an account, you can use it as an AI provider instead of Zo's built-in credits.

## Setup

1. Open the terminal on your Zo and run `gemini` to start the login flow

2. Follow the prompts to authenticate with your Google account

<Info>
  You may need to restart `gemini` by hitting Ctrl+C in the terminal after logging in if it is unresponsive and opening it again.
</Info>

3. Go to **Settings > AI Providers** and enable **Gemini CLI**

4. Select your preferred model

5. Select "Gemini CLI" from the model picker in chat and you're set
